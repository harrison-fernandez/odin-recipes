Borga

description

Burger is like lasagna, it is a sandwich of bread, cheese, meat, lettuce, tomato, and onion.

You only need to cook the meat patty and then assemble the other ingredients. Cook the patty in a nice cast iron skillet.

ingredients

  • Bread
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • onion
    •

    steps

    1. grab a skillet, put heat on
    2. season your meat patty
    3. fry the patty on the skillet to your desired temp - medium rare if possible
    4. take patty off heat
    5. put patty on a piece of bread, put cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion on top
    6. eat
