Lasagny

description

Lasagna is a set of delicious layers of pasta, cheese, and sauce. I recommend multiple layers of repeating the same ingredients. You can add a layer of ground meat too.

You put it in the oven for a while and it slowly cooks.

ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Pasta again
    •

    steps

    1. grab a tray
    2. preheat your oven
    3. put pasta, meat, cheese in tray
    4. repeat step 3 until you reach the top of the tray
    5. put tray in oven
    6. take out when ready
