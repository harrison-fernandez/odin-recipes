Pancake

description

Pancake is a yummy breakfast food, best served with syrup.

You only need to add water or milk to the pre made batter.

ingredients

  • water or milk
  • pancake batter
  • pan
  • fork
  • syrup
    •

    steps

    1. grab a skillet, put heat on
    2. add milk to the dry mix
    3. cook mix in skillet
    4. flip pancake
    5. put on plate and put syrup on top
    6. eat
